Not bad for about an hour's drive from Melbourne. You can both park your car and start the walk from Mackenzies Flat Picnic Area.

<MapViewer gpxUrl="/tracks/lederderg-gorge-circuit.gpx" />

[Graham's Dam](https://swimmingholeheaven.com/vic-lerderderg-grahams.htm) is just before the loop ascent and descent, which means you can go for a swim after (or before) the climb. The water was a too low for us, so we swam about 15 minutes further south.

The loop ascent itself is pretty vertical, so don't skimp on shoes.
